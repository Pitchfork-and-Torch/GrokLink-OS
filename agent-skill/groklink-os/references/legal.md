(or are explicitly authorized to operate on).

Unauthorized access to RF / IR / RFID / NFC / access-control / vehicle systems
may be a crime. Authors accept **no liability** for misuse.

- **Not a medical device.**
- TX, GPIO drive, contact, and system actions are **default-deny**.
- Education session phrase:

```text
I_WILL_USE_ONLY_AUTHORIZED_TARGETS
```

interception, jamming, access-control bypass, or unattended transmission.
